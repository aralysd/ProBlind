About

Tom Gilbert has been in the blind selling business for over 10 years. Over these years Tom and his son Zachary saw a need in the local and surrounding areas for blind services. This is when the father son duo realized they wanted to make that happen.

All too often customers that buy blinds will over the years replace them due to the blinds being dirty, or broken. This is where Zach and Tom come in.

With their ever growing knowledge with blinds, the two decided to take some online courses to build on that blind knowledge and become something more. Tom became Hunter Douglas Certified so that they could better serve their existing and new customers with Blind services.

Now when you come into Gilbert's Carpets Plus Color Tile to purchase new blinds, you now have the options to have them professionally installed by Zach and Tom. Also, no longer is it needed to throw out those blinds when they get broken or dirty.

Let Tom and Zach take the stress out of blinds for you!
