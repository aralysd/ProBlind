Ultrasonic

Ultrasonic cleaners began being used in industry around 1950 and has since grown over the years. In those same years, there has been many people selling blinds in commercial and residential buildings, but who is cleaning and repairing them?

Blinds act as filters. They catch things like, road grime, dust, mold spores, and smoke. Not only do they collect things from outdoors, but they work in collecting things in your home as well. Cooking oders, grease, nicotine, and bacteria from the cold and flu viruses are also filtered through blinds and shades. Blinds help in aiding for a healthier home, so why not have them cleaned to keep that home a healthy one?

The ultrasonic machine uses cavitation bubbles induced by high frequency pressure sound waves to agitate a liquid. When adding a cleaning solution to the water, those little bubbles clean the blinds as well as removing any oders that may have been there.
